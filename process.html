My Data Analysis Process

I believe data analysis is more than just numbers. It’s storytelling with structure. My process follows five guiding stages:

  1. Ask → Define the business problem clearly.
  2. Prepare → Collect, clean, and understand the data sources.
  3. Analyze → Use statistical and exploratory techniques.
  4. Visualize → Communicate findings with clarity and design.
  5. Share → Present insights with recommendations and actions.

This framework helps me stay consistent, transparent, and impactful when delivering insights.
