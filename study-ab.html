Summary

Designed a test with CUPED to reduce variance; delivered clearer read with smaller sample.

Problem

Evaluate impact of a new onboarding flow on week‑1 activation.

Data

  • Sources: App event logs; user cohorts
  • Timeframe: 8 weeks
  • Size & key fields: 1.2M users; metrics: activation_rate, retention_d7

Approach

CUPED covariate from pre‑treatment behavior; delta method for CI.

theta = cov(Y0, X)/var(X)
Y_adj = Y - theta*X

Results

Detected +2.1 pp activation with p < 0.05; practical uplift confirmed in ramp.
