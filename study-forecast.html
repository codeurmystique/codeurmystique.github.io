Case Study 3: Fitness Tracker Marketing

Dataset: FitBit Fitness Tracker Dataset (public dataset on Kaggle – licensed for educational use).

Business Context

Bellabeat (fictional women’s wellness company) wanted to leverage fitness-tracker data to inform marketing strategies and increase user engagement.

Tools Used

  • SQL (data cleaning and querying)
  • Tableau (dashboards & visualization)

Key Insights

  • Users with consistent sleep and step patterns were more engaged with Bellabeat’s app.
  • Engagement was higher among users who tracked their wellness across multiple categories (activity, calories, heart rate).
  • Weekend activity levels dropped sharply compared to weekdays.

Recommendations

  • Launch campaigns encouraging holistic wellness tracking (not just steps).
  • Personalized reminders for sleep and recovery goals.
  • Weekend activity challenges to boost app engagement.
