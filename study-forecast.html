Case Study 3: Bellabeat Marketing Analysis

Business Problem: A fitness-tracker company, **Bellabeat**, wanted to analyze user data from a public dataset to gain insights into how people use their smart devices. These insights would be used to inform Bellabeatâ€™s marketing strategy and guide future business decisions.

Tools Used

  • •

    SQL (Data cleaning and analysis)

  • •

    Tableau (Dashboards and visualizations)

Key Insights

  • •

    Users who log data for all three metrics (activity, sleep, and weight) are more engaged and use the device for a longer period.

  • •

    There is a positive correlation between active time and logged sleep data, suggesting that more active users are also more conscious about their sleep habits.

  • •

    Users who use the tracker less than 3 times a week tend to have a higher sedentary rate.

Recommendations

  • •

    Develop a feature in the app to encourage and gamify the logging of all three metrics (activity, sleep, and weight).

  • •

    Launch a marketing campaign highlighting the link between activity and sleep, positioning Bellabeat as a holistic wellness solution.

  • •

    Introduce a "weekly challenge" to incentivize low-activity users to reach a minimum usage goal and reduce sedentary behavior.
